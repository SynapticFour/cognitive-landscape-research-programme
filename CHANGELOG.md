## Release tagging convention

| Tag pattern | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| `clrp-vYYYY.M` | Programme release (e.g. `clrp-v2026.1`) |
| `CLRP-NNN-vX.Y.Z` | Individual accepted document release (optional, for major spec versions) |

Releases are cut when stewards determine a citable snapshot is needed (typically quarterly or after Accepted document batches).
