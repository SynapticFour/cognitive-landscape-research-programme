# CLRP-007: Non-Diagnostic Commitment

## Abstract

CLRP **rejects** use of landscape coordinates for clinical diagnosis, gatekeeping, or institutional sorting. This is a programme commitment (L0), not a temporary implementation limitation.

## 1. Commitment

Instruments and systems within CLRP:

- **Must not** present outputs as medical or psychiatric diagnosis
- **Must not** gate access to healthcare assessment, education, employment, or services
- **Must not** replace professional evaluation
- **Must not** use categorical disorder labels as primary outputs

## 2. Appropriate uses

- Self-understanding with informed consent
- Research with ethics approval
- Education about dimensional models
- Simulation and methods development
- Aggregate cohort research without individual identification

## 3. Inappropriate uses

| Use | Status |
|-----|--------|
| ADHD/autism screening | Forbidden |
| Hiring / firing decisions | Forbidden |
| School placement or streaming | Forbidden without separate validated process outside CLRP |
| Insurance or legal evidence | Forbidden |
| Coercive administration | Forbidden |

## 4. Language requirements

User-facing materials should:

- Avoid clinical terminology as result labels
- Surface uncertainty and non-diagnostic disclaimers
- Not imply treatment recommendations from coordinates

## 5. Integrator responsibilities

CLRP documents bind **programme participants**. Deployers of conformant instruments remain responsible for:

- Local lawful basis and consent
- Privacy and retention
- Facilitator training in non-diagnostic framing

## 6. Reporting misuse

Report ethical misuse to [contact@synapticfour.com](mailto:contact@synapticfour.com). Security issues: [SECURITY.md](../SECURITY.md).

## 7. Non-goals

- Providing legal advice per jurisdiction
- Certifying third-party deployments as "safe"
