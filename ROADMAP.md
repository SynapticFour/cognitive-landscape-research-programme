# Research Roadmap

This roadmap covers **programme-level** milestones for the Cognitive Landscape Research Programme (CLRP). Implementation-specific roadmaps remain in CLM, PCMS, and other repositories but must align with Accepted CLRP documents.

**Horizon:** 2026–2029 (initial 3-year plan)  
**Review cycle:** Quarterly steward review; annual public revision

---

## Phase 0 — Foundation (2026 Q3–Q4)

**Goal:** Establish CLRP as the authoritative, citable programme home.

| Milestone | Target | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Publish CLRP repository with governance stack | 2026 Q3 | In progress |
| CLRP-000 through CLRP-009 in Draft | 2026 Q3 | In progress |
| CLRP-000, CLRP-002, CLRP-007 → Proposed | 2026 Q4 | Planned |
| First Zenodo release (`clrp-v2026.1`) | 2026 Q4 | Planned |
| Ecosystem cross-links (CLM, PCMS, website) | 2026 Q4 | Planned |
| Migrate shared ethics/validation framing from implementation repos | 2026 Q4 | Planned |

**Exit criteria:** External reader can understand programme purpose without reading code; at least three CLRP documents in Proposed state.

---

## Phase 1 — Vocabulary and evidence framework (2027)

**Goal:** Stabilise core definitions and validation standards.

| Milestone | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| CLRP-002 (Vocabulary) → Accepted | 2027 Q1 |
| CLRP-003 (Measurement) → Accepted | 2027 Q2 |
| CLRP-006 (Evidence and Validation) → Accepted | 2027 Q2 |
| CLRP-005 (Layer Separation) → Accepted | 2027 Q3 |
| First validation report published (`validation/`) | 2027 Q3 |
| Bibliography curated (≥50 core references) | 2027 Q4 |
| Historical influences document set (≥5 entries) | 2027 Q4 |

**Research activities (linked implementations):**

- PCMS Phase 1 pilot (N≈150/locale) — evidence feeds CLRP validation series
- CLM bridge stability and golden-vector conformance — reported as CLRP-TR

**Exit criteria:** Accepted vocabulary and validation framework; at least one published validation report with honest limitations.

---

## Phase 2 — Cultural programme and structural evidence (2028)

**Goal:** Move from single-locale prototypes toward a defensible cross-cultural programme.

| Milestone | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| CLRP-008 (Cultural Adaptation) → Accepted | 2028 Q1 |
| Cross-locale adaptation case studies (≥2 locales) | 2028 Q2 |
| Measurement invariance study design (pre-registered) | 2028 Q2 |
| CLRP-001 (Discovering the Landscape) → Accepted | 2028 Q3 |
| Structural calibration evidence summary | 2028 Q4 |
| Open questions register reviewed and prioritised | 2028 Q4 |

**Exit criteria:** Documented adaptation methodology; no unsupported cross-locale comparability claims in programme materials.

---

## Phase 3 — Longitudinal and explanatory programme (2029)

**Goal:** Establish evidence standards for dynamics, trajectories, and interpretation boundaries.

| Milestone | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Longitudinal validation protocol (Accepted or CLRP-TR) | 2029 Q1 |
| CLRP-004 (Epistemic Commitments) → Accepted | 2029 Q2 |
| Simulation-vs-observation reporting standard | 2029 Q2 |
| CLRP-009 (Implementations) → Accepted | 2029 Q3 |
| Programme methods paper (external journal) | 2029 Q4 |
| `clrp-v2029.1` major release | 2029 Q4 |

**Exit criteria:** Clear programme-level methods publication; conformance model for implementations; longitudinal evidence plan operational.

---

## Beyond 2029 (directional, not committed)

These are **research directions**, not scheduled deliverables:

- Hierarchical trait architectures (e.g. micro-trait programmes) as extensions via proposals
- Cohort-level aggregate research standards (privacy-preserving)
- Integration with open science infrastructure (OSF, RO-Crate programme metadata)
- Independent replication by external research groups
- Translation of Accepted CLRP documents (locale-specific forks with attribution)

---

## Non-goals (programme-wide)

The roadmap explicitly excludes:

- Clinical diagnostic approval or FDA/CE marking pathways
- High-stakes employment or educational screening standards
- Replacing peer review with repository acceptance
- Centralising all cognitive-science research under CLRP branding
