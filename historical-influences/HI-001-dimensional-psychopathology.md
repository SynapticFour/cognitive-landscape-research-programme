# HI-001: Dimensional psychopathology (HiTOP)

**Status:** Draft influence note

## Source

Kotov, R. et al. (2017). The Hierarchical Taxonomy of Psychopathology (HiTOP): A dimensional alternative to traditional nosologies. *Journal of Abnormal Psychology*, 126(4), 454–477.

## Contribution to CLRP

- **Continuous structure** over categorical diagnosis for describing variation
- **Hierarchical organisation** of dimensions at multiple granularities
- Emphasis on **empirical discovery** rather than committee-defined labels

## What CLRP does not adopt

- Focus on psychopathology as primary domain (CLRP is broader cognitive diversity)
- Any implication that dimensional scores replace clinical care
- HiTOP-specific factor structure as CLRP's dimension set
