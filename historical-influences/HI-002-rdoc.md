# HI-002: Research Domain Criteria (RDoC)

**Status:** Draft influence note

## Source

Insel, T. et al. (2010). Research Domain Criteria (RDoC): Toward a new classification framework for research on mental disorders. *American Journal of Psychiatry*, 167(7), 748–751.

## Contribution to CLRP

- **Multi-level analysis** (genes → circuits → behaviour → self-report)
- Encouragement to study **dimensions** spanning health and variation
- Separation of **research constructs** from clinical nosology

## What CLRP does not adopt

- RDoC matrix as exhaustive ontology
- Neurobiological reductionism as programme default
- NIMH institutional positions post-2024 as binding on CLRP
