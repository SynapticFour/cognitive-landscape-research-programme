# HI-003: Continuous trait theory

**Status:** Draft influence note

## Sources (representative)

- Costa, P. T., & McCrae, R. R. (1992). *Revised NEO Personality Inventory* manual.
- DeYoung, C. G. et al. (2007). Between facets and domains: 10 aspects of the Big Five. *Journal of Personality and Social Psychology*.
- Modern psychometric literature on IRT and bifactor models.

## Contribution to CLRP

- **Trait continua** as natural language for individual differences
- **Facet-level granularity** informing multi-dimensional instruments
- Statistical tools for reliability and structure testing (applied per CLRP-006)

## What CLRP does not adopt

- Big Five / NEO as sole or privileged dimension set
- Trait stability claims without longitudinal CLRP-VR evidence
- Personality type popularisations (e.g. MBTI-style categories)
